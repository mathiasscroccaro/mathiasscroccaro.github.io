Short résumé

Hello, my name is Mathias!

I am from Curitiba, a city in the south of Brazil. I was born in 1994 and since I was a child I have disassembled electronic equipment, electric toys and have an interest in its operation.

In 2017 I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Electronic Engineering from UTFPR, campus Toledo. During my undergraduate studies I had a solid training in fundamentals of programming, especially applied to embedded systems. I also had contact with disciplines that dealt with hardware application: digital and analog circuits, sensing and signal conditioning.

In the sequence, I entered the Master's program in Electrical Engineering at the State University of Campinas (Unicamp) and was able to complete it in 2020. I developed my master's research at the Laboratory of Solutions in Electronics and Radio Frequency (LSERF), under the title "Development of a telemetry device for water meters under the IoT perspective: Studies on energy generation and consumption". During this period, I had the opportunity to study RF electronic circuits and wave guides, in addition to deepening the development of embedded systems and processing of sensor signals.

I took the following graduate courses:

  • Acquisition and conditioning of sensor signals;
  • Digital signal processing;
  • Manufacturing and Characterization of MOS Devices (Clean Room Practices);
  • Manufacturing and Characterization of MOS Devices (Manufacturing Theory);
  • Introduction to RF microelectronic circuits;
  • History and philosophy of electrical engineering and computing;
  • (Listener) Guidance and radiation of RF waves;
